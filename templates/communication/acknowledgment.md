# Ticket Acknowledgment Template

Use this when you first pick up a ticket to let the customer know you're on it.

---

## Standard Acknowledgment

Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for contacting Datadog Support. I'm [Your Name] and I'll be assisting you with this issue.

I've reviewed your ticket and understand that [brief restatement of the issue]. I'm going to [what you're going to do next - investigate, test, review logs, etc.].

I'll update you within [timeframe] with my findings or next steps.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
Datadog Support

---

## When You Need More Information

Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for reaching out. I've reviewed your ticket and I'm working on this issue.

To help me investigate further, could you please provide:

- [Specific piece of information 1]
- [Specific piece of information 2]
- [Specific piece of information 3]

Once I have this information, I'll be able to [what you'll do with it].

Thanks for your patience!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
Datadog Support

---

## High-Priority/Urgent Issues

Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for contacting us. I understand this is impacting your [production environment / monitoring / critical systems] and I'm treating this as a priority.

I'm starting my investigation now and will provide an update within [shorter timeframe, e.g., 2 hours] even if I don't have a full resolution yet.

In the meantime, if the situation escalates or you have additional information, please don't hesitate to update this ticket.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
Datadog Support

# Escalation Notice Template

Use when escalating a case to engineering.

---

## Standard Escalation to Engineering

Hi [Customer Name],

I've been investigating this issue and want to ensure you get the best possible resolution.

I'm escalating this to our Engineering team who specialize in [product area / type of issue]. They'll be able to perform a deeper investigation into [specific aspect].

**What happens next:**
- An engineer will review the case within [timeframe, typically 24-48 hours]
- I'll stay involved and keep you updated
- You may be asked for additional technical details

I'll update you as soon as I have news from the team.

Thank you for your patience!

Best regards,
[Your Name]

---

## Escalation for Suspected Bug

Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for providing all that information. Based on my investigation, I believe this may be a product issue that requires our engineering team's attention.

**What I've Found:**
[Summary of investigation findings]

**Next Steps:**
I'm creating an escalation ticket with our engineering team with all the details from your case. This will ensure the issue is:
- Properly investigated by our product engineers
- Tracked for resolution
- Prioritized appropriately

**Timeline:**
- Engineering review: [typical timeframe]
- I'll provide updates at least every [frequency, e.g., "every 3 business days"]
- If there are any workarounds in the meantime, I'll let you know immediately

**Your Case:**
Your support case will remain open and I'll keep you informed of all progress.

Thank you for bringing this to our attention and for your patience as we investigate further.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
